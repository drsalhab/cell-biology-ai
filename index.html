Exploring Cells Through AI

Red Blood Cell (Erythrocyte)

  • Unique biconcave shape (like a donut with a thin center)
  • No nucleus - maximizes space for hemoglobin
  • Size: 7-8 micrometers in diameter
  • Flexible membrane for squeezing through capillaries
  • Function: Oxygen transport throughout the body

Plant Cell

  • Rectangular/brick-like shape with rigid cell wall
  • Contains large central vacuole
  • Size: 20-100 micrometers
  • Has chloroplasts for photosynthesis
  • Function: Food production through photosynthesis

Bacterial Cell

  • Rod-shaped (bacillus form)
  • No membrane-bound nucleus (prokaryotic)
  • Size: 2-3 micrometers
  • Single circular DNA
  • Function: Various roles in human health and disease
